Brundall Bullfinches Move to Thorpe High 3G Pitch as New Home Ground

January 27, 2025

We are excited to announce that we have a new 3G home pitch for the upcoming 2025-2026 season. After hearing the news that Norfolk Sunday League Premier Division side Vale Longo had folded, we saw an opportunity to move to a closer facility and are pleased to say that it will be our official home ground next season.

We have had a lot of fun up at St. Faiths and some of the guys will certainly miss slide tackling in the mud, but at least there will still be opportunities to do that in away games for now.

There are of course pros and cons to 3G and grass, but the team has unanimously voted in favour of the move. One big positive for us is that in theory, we shouldn't have weather-related cancellations next season. As mentioned earlier, it’s also a fair bit closer for the majority of the team!
